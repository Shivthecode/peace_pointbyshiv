Top Destinations of India

Mathura : City in Uttar Pradesh

Famous For : Mathura is renowned as the birthplace of Lord Krishna, a major deity in Hinduism. It's considered one of the seven sacred cities for Hindus, known as Sapta Puri.

Total Tourist : Mathura saw over 65.39 million tourist visits in a years

Best Time to Visit: October to March is the best time to visit Mathura Uttar Pradesh. For experience the best of the city's culture and indulge in the spirit of Lord Krishna, the best time to visit is during Krishna Janmashthami.

For reaching Mathura from other cities:
Train: Mathura Junction is well-connected by rail.
Road: Mathura is easily accessible via Yamuna Expressway, and Delhi-Agra highway.
Air: The nearest airports are in Agra (71 km)

Top activity: Shri Krishna Janmasthan Temple,Vishram Ghat,Yamuna Aarti,Dwarkadhish Temple,Mathura Museum,Wildlife SOS - Elephant Conservation and Care Center,Kusum Sarovar,Shopping in Tilak Dwar Market.

Vrindavan : City in Uttar Pradesh

Famous For :Vrindavan stands as a prominent religious centre in Braj Bhoomi and attracts devotees with its rich history. It was here that the famous saint Vallabhacharya and master of Indian classical music Swami Haridas stayed. Even Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, a revered saint, visited Braj Bhoomi on his pilgrimage

Total Tourist : 16 million tourists, with 57,000+ being international visitors.

Best Time to Visit:The best time to travel to Vrindavan is during the winter months, from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and comfortable for sightseeing and temple visits.

For reaching Vrindavan from other cities:
Train: Mathura Junction approx 20km form Vrindavan.
Road: Vrindavan via Mathura is easily accessible via Yamuna Expressway, and Delhi-Agra highway.
Air: The nearest airports are in Agra (91 km)

Top activity: Banke Bihari Temple and ISKCON Vrindavan, witnessing the Yamuna Aarti, exploring local markets, and experiencing the Holi festival,Nidhivan (Rang Mahal),Yamuna Aarti,Prem Mandir.

Taj Mahal : Place in Uttar Pradesh

Famous For :The Taj Mahal is famous for its exquisite white marble mausoleum, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and its status as a global symbol of love. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal

Total Tourist :7-8 million visitors per year. On a typical day, it sees around 22,000 visitors, but this can drastically increase to over 40,000 on busy days.

Best Time to Visit:October to March, particularly early mornings or late afternoons or Consider Full Moon Nights.

For reaching Taj Mahal from other cities:
By Train: Agra Cantt Railway Station is a major railway hub with connections to Delhi and other cities.
Road: Agra is accessible via national highways and has regular bus services.
Air:Agra Airport (AGR) is well-connected to major Indian cities.

Top activity: Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Mehtab Bagh, enjoying a heritage walk, and experiencing the local cuisine and markets.

Mawsynram : Town in Meghalaya

Famous For :Mawsynram is famous for being one of the wettest places on Earth, known for its exceptionally high rainfall. It receives an average annual rainfall of 11,873 mm

Total Tourist :Mawsynram is known for being the wettest place on Earth, there's no specific data on the average tourist visits.

Best Time to Visit:The best time to visit Mawsynram, the wettest place on Earth, is during the summer months (April to June). While the monsoon season (June to September) brings the area its unique character, the high rainfall can make travel difficult.

For reaching Mawsynram from other cities:
By Train: Guwahati Railway Station is the nearest railway station to Mawsynram.
Road: Mawsynram is approximately 152 kilometers from Guwahati, and the drive takes about 4 hours and Mawsynram is about 60 kilometers southwest of Shillong.
Air: Shillong Airport, also known as Umroi Airport, is the nearest airspace from this town.

Top activity: Mawjymbuin Cave, Shiva Linga formation, trek to the green plateaus, and witness the power of the Seven Sisters Waterfall. You can also experience the unique lifestyle adapted to the heavy rainfall, including the iconic living root bridges.

Darjeeling : City in West Bengal

Famous For :Tea and Darjeeling are synonymous,Darjeeling Tea is world renowned for its flavour, which is unequaled by other tea producing areas not only of India but also of the world. Darjeeling still manufactures the tea by the original methods known as the 'orthodox' method.

Total Tourist :Presently around 6,00,000 Indian tourists and 30,000 foreign nationals visit Darjeeling annually.

Best Time to Visit:April to June when the weather is pleasant.The months of July to August welcome the showers.

For reaching Darjeeling from other cities:
By Train: Darjeeling Himalayan Railway Station (88 K.M. from New Jalpaiguri Railway Station) the two closest railway stations are Siliguri and New Jalpaiguri.
Road: By road from Siliguri.
Air: The nearest airport to Darjeeling is Bagdogra, close to Siliguri.

Top activity: Tiger Hill,Happy Valley Tea Estate,Batasia Loop,Peace Pagoda,Rock Garden and Ganga Maya Park,Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train),Trekking,River Rafting,Paragliding,Japanese Temple,Monasteries.

Varanasi : City in Uttar Pardesh

Famous For :Sarees, handicrafts, textiles, Toys, ornaments, metal work, clay and wood work, leaf and fibre crafts,the most sacred city for Hindus, and the oldest living city in the world.

Total Tourist :100,000-150,000 tourists visit Varanasi.

Best Time to Visit:November to February. During this time, the weather is cool and pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 5°C to 20°C

For reaching Varanasi from other cities:
By Train: Varanasi Junction (BSB) is the main railway station serving the city.
Road: via Purvanchal Expressway.
Air: You can fly into Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport (VNS), located about 26 kilometers from the city center in Babatpur.

Top activity: experiencing the Ganga Aarti ceremony at Dashashwamedh Ghat,Ganga Aarti,Boat Ride on the Ganges,Kashi Vishwanath Temple,Sarnath,Old City Exploration,Sunrise and Sunset at the Ghats,Banaras Hindu University,Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple.
